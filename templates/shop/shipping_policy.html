🔍 How Your Order Gets to You
1. Order Confirmed
You receive an email confirmation immediately. Your order is queued in our fulfilment system.
2. Processing & Packing
Our warehouse team picks, quality-checks, and securely packs your items. Typically within 4–6 hours during business hours.
3. Dispatched with Carrier
Your package is handed to the carrier and you receive a tracking number via email. You can also track via your TrendMart dashboard.
4. Out for Delivery
Your local carrier scans the package. You may receive a push notification or SMS with a delivery window.
5. Delivered 🎉
Package arrives at your door! Leave a review to help other shoppers — and earn loyalty points.
