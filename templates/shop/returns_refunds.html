Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns, guaranteed. Shop with total confidence — we've got you covered.

30
Day Returns
No questions asked

Changed your mind? Not happy with your purchase? Simply return any eligible item within 30 days of receiving it for a full refund or exchange — no awkward questions.

📋 How to Return in 4 Easy Steps
1
📱
Start Your Return
Go to your Orders page and click "Return Item" next to the product.
2
📝
Tell Us Why
Select a reason (e.g. wrong size, not as described) — takes 30 seconds.
3
📦
Pack & Send
Use the original packaging if possible. Print the prepaid return label we email you.
4
💳
Get Refunded
Once we receive your return, your refund is processed within 2–5 business days.
✅ What Can Be Returned?
✅ Eligible for Return
  • Unused items in original condition
  • Items with original tags/packaging
  • Defective or damaged goods
  • Wrong item received
  • Items that don't match the listing
  • Sealed electronics (unopened)
  • Clothing & footwear (unworn)
  • Beauty products (sealed/unopened)
❌ Not Eligible
  • Items used, washed, or worn
  • Opened software / digital downloads
  • Personalised or custom orders
  • Perishable goods (food, flowers)
  • Items missing original packaging
  • Returned after 30 days
  • Hazardous materials
  • Intimate apparel for hygiene reasons
💡
Tip: Photograph items before returning
Take a photo of the item and packaging before sealing the return parcel. This protects you in the rare event of a dispute about the item's condition.
